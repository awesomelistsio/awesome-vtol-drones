> A curated list of awesome resources, research, and companies related to Vertical Take-Off and Landing (VTOL) drones.

VTOL drones combine the benefits of multirotor and fixed-wing aircraft, offering vertical takeoff/landing with extended flight range and efficiency. This list features high-quality tools, platforms, academic research, and commercial applications for VTOL UAV systems.

## Overview & Concepts

- [VTOL UAV Basics – UAV Coach](https://uavcoach.com/vtol/) – A beginner’s guide to VTOL drone technology.
- [Fixed-Wing VTOL vs. Traditional Drones](https://www.tealdrone.com/blog/vtol-vs-fixed-wing-drones) – Pros and cons of hybrid platforms.
- [Understanding VTOL Configurations](https://www.auvsi.org/industry-news/exploring-configurations-vtol-aircraft) – Quadplane, tilt-rotor, tail-sitter, and more.

## VTOL Drone Platforms

- [WingtraOne](https://wingtra.com/) – High-precision VTOL drone for surveying and mapping.
- [Quantum Systems Trinity F90+](https://www.quantum-systems.com/trinity/) – Long-range VTOL mapping drone.
- [Vertical Technologies DeltaQuad](https://www.deltaquad.com/) – VTOL UAV for surveillance, mapping, and delivery.
- [AeroVironment JUMP 20](https://www.avinc.com/uas/view/jump-20) – Military-grade VTOL ISR platform.
- [eVTOL News – Aircraft Directory](https://evtol.news/aircraft/) – Extensive directory of electric VTOL aircraft.

## Open-Source Projects

- [PX4 VTOL Support](https://docs.px4.io/main/en/flight_modes/transition.html) – PX4 Autopilot documentation for VTOL transitions.
- [ArduPilot VTOL Docs](https://ardupilot.org/plane/docs/guide-vtol-quadplanes.html) – ArduPilot’s hybrid drone guide.
- [OpenVToL](https://github.com/openvtol) – Community-driven VTOL drone development (experimental).

## Flight Control Systems

- [CubePilot](https://www.cubepilot.org/) – Widely used flight controller with VTOL compatibility.
- [PX4 Autopilot](https://px4.io/) – Robust open-source flight stack for advanced VTOL operations.
- [ArduPilot](https://ardupilot.org/) – Full-featured autopilot suite with VTOL-specific support.

## Design & Simulation Tools

- [UAVCAN – For Hybrid VTOL Communication](https://uavcan.org/) – Lightweight protocol for UAV component communication.
- [QGroundControl](https://docs.qgroundcontrol.com/en/) – Ground station software for mission planning.
- [XFLR5](http://www.xflr5.tech/xflr5.htm) – Analysis tool for airfoil and wing configurations.
- [Gazebo Simulator](https://gazebosim.org/) – 3D robotics simulation with VTOL integration via PX4.

## VTOL Research

- [NASA VTOL Studies](https://nari.arc.nasa.gov/vtol) – Research on vertical flight and hybrid aircraft.
- [Journal of Intelligent & Robotic Systems – VTOL Papers](https://link.springer.com/journal/10846) – Peer-reviewed research.
- [IEEE Papers on VTOL UAVs](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/Xplore/home.jsp) – Search for VTOL drone design, control, and autonomy.

## Regulations & Standards

- [FAA UAS Regulations](https://www.faa.gov/uas) – U.S. rules for commercial and hybrid UAV operations.
- [EASA Drone Rules](https://www.easa.europa.eu/en/domains/civil-drones-rpas) – EU regulations for drone platforms.
- [Transport Canada – RPAS](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/drone-safety) – Canadian RPAS rules including fixed-wing VTOL.

## Commercial Applications

- **Aerial Surveying** – VTOLs are widely used for topographic, agricultural, and infrastructure mapping.
- **Surveillance & ISR** – Used in law enforcement and military for persistent aerial observation.
- **Cargo & Delivery** – VTOL configurations offer extended range and vertical access for package delivery.
- **Disaster Response** – Hybrid drones offer the flexibility to access remote or damaged areas.
